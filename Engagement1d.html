Why running?

The nature is a good giver of oxygen and vitamins in the body. When I run I feel energized and ready for the day.

My influences
  1. My parents has always been my biggest cheerer when it comes to our health in the family. I grew up with asthma and therefore has a weak lungs. Sure, medicine helps but making my body healthy will make me live more longer :) My dad would always make me practice breathing exercises to make my lungs strong. I remember, before I went to leave for mission he would come with me and run on days that he isn't working.
  2. My mission companions has always been hard worker and great at exercising so we would encourage each other to run every morning for 30 minutes non-stop. It felt tiring but refreshing. It made us stronger and healthier as we go out and serve the people for the rest of our days.
